**What the GENIUS Act and its regulators require, by frequency**

- **Weekly** — the OCC's proposed reporting form (PS-01) requires issuers to submit a confidential weekly report on payment stablecoin activity and reserves.
- **Monthly** — issuers must publicly disclose reserve composition on their website, and get that disclosure examined by an accounting firm, every month (Sec. 4(a)(1)(C), 4(a)(3)(A)).
- **Quarterly** — the OCC's proposed PS-02 form requires a quarterly report of condition and income.
- **Annually** — large issuers (>$50B) must produce a full audited financial statement (Sec. 4(a)(10)), and issuers must submit an annual AML/sanctions certification (Sec. 5(i)).
- **Continuous/on-demand** — regulators can request reports or examine an issuer at any time (Sec. 6(a)), this isn't scheduled, it's discretionary.
